Jephte's Tutoring Services

The goal of the tutoring is to help students overcome academic challenges and lead them to autonomous or independent learning.

It is a special kind of teaching that is different from the teaching performed by teachers, friends, and parents. We offer students one-on-one attention, individualized explanation, and a chance to ask as many question as they like (some of which they may not ask people they know) in a nonjudgmental atmosphere. But remember, the goal of overcoming academic challenges must be realized by promoting academic independence in the tutee and not dependence on the tutor.
